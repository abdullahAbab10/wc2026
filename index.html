About This Guide
This guide covers all 104 matches of the 2026 FIFA World Cup — the biggest tournament in history with 48 nations across the USA, Mexico, and Canada. All kick-off times have been converted to Arabia Standard Time (AST, UTC+3), which is Saudi Arabia’s local time. AST is 7 hours ahead of US Eastern Daylight Time (EDT), so a 3pm EDT game is 10pm AST. Games kicking off after midnight AST are marked 🌙 Late Night. No daylight saving applies in KSA.

Tournament at a Glance
The 2026 FIFA World Cup is the largest in history: 48 nations, 104 matches, across 16 venues in 3 countries over 39 days. Saudi Arabia 🇸🇦 is in Group H alongside Spain, Uruguay, and Cape Verde. The knockout format introduces a brand-new Round of 32 for the first time ever. A team must win 8 matches to lift the trophy. The champion will be crowned on 19 July at 23:00 AST. 🌙
