Tournament at a Glance
The 2026 FIFA World Cup is the largest in history: 48 nations, 104 matches, 16 venues across 3 countries over 39 days. Saudi Arabia 🇸🇦 is in Group H alongside Spain, Uruguay, and Cape Verde. First-ever Round of 32. A team needs 8 wins to lift the trophy. The final is 19 July at 23:00 AST. 🌙
